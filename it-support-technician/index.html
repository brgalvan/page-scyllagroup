IT Support Technician

As a Support Technician at The Scylla Group, you will diagnose hardware and software issues, deploy and configure equipment, maintain equipment, and solve issues for customers while working in a fast-paced, team environment that strives to keep up with new technologies and trends.

The ideal candidate should have demonstrable experience troubleshooting hardware and software issues, prioritizing and escalating tasks, and a desire to expand your skillset. You will work in a team environment where you will be expected to ask questions and contribute to conversations. We are a managed services provider and support a large client base.Â  All team members work in a variety of infrastructures and with a mixture of technology products on any given day. If you are looking for an opportunity to expand your skillsets, keep current, and work with a highly capable team of peers, then this may be the opportunity for you.

This is a part-time or full-time position depending on the candidate. Job duties will mostly be carried out in our Warrenton, VA office, but will occasionally be conducted at client sites (local area).

The Scylla Group is an interdisciplinary agency versed in business strategy, graphic design, and information technology. We consistently work in the latest emerging technologies providing our clients with first-class solutions.

Ideal candidates should feel comfortable performing the following

  • Working with Microsoft Active Directory and Group Policy
  • Setting up accounts and workstations
  • Monitoring performance and maintain systems according to requirements
  • Updating systems and maintaining patch levels
  • Troubleshooting network connectivity (LAN, WAN, DNS, DHCP, TCP/IP, WiFi, etc.)
  • Troubleshooting issues remotely, over-the-phone and on-site
  • Communicating with customers / end-users

DESIRED SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE

  • Strong problem-solving skills
  • Strong organizational skills
  • Excellent interpersonal and communication skills (both written and verbal)
  • Experience with Microsoft Windows 8.1-10
  • Experience with Microsoft Office (2013-2019 / 365)
